Writing Task: Academic Discussion

Instructions: Your professor is teaching a class on sociology. Write a post responding to the professor's question. In your response, you should express and support your personal opinion and contribute to the discussion in your own words. (Minimum 100 words recommended).
Professor Dr. Elizabeth Miller

Next week, we will be discussing the impact of remote work on urban development. Some argue that the rise of working from home will lead to the "death of the city" as people move to rural areas. Others believe cities will simply evolve into social hubs. What is your perspective? Do you think the trend toward remote work is ultimately positive or negative for society?

Kelly

I think it's mostly positive. Remote work reduces the need for long commutes, which is better for the environment and gives people more time with their families. Even if cities lose some office workers, they will become more affordable for artists and young people.

Andrew

I disagree with Kelly. I think cities rely on the daily influx of workers to support local businesses like cafes and transit systems. If everyone stays home, the economic heart of the city will stop beating, leading to higher crime and urban decay.
